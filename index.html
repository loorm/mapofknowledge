How it works

Three steps.
A lifetime of learning.

1
Find a concept
Navigate the knowledge graph by zooming, panning, and filtering. Use curriculum filters — Estonian Main School, your own knowledge profile — or simply explore until something catches your eye.
2
Learn it deeply
Click "Learn this" and the adaptive tutor takes over. It explains the concept in short bytes, demonstrates with worked examples, then gives you practice problems — adjusting in real time to how you respond.
3
It lands in your Passport
Completed units are recorded in your Learner Passport — with your score, the date, and a cryptographic credential you can share. The map node changes colour. Your knowledge becomes visible.
